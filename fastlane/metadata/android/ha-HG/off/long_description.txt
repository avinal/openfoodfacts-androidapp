*** Contribute to Product Transparency ***
- Open Products Facts is a products database made by everyone, for everyone.
- You can use it to make better choices, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.
- Open Products Facts is a non-profit project developed by thousands of volunteers from around the world. Muna da ayyuka masu ban sha'awa da yawa waɗanda zaku iya ba da gudummawarsu ta hanyoyi daban-daban.
- You can start contributing today by adding your first product.

*** Get the Facts ***
- Open Products Facts helps you to make sense of the fine print on products labels. You will also be able to find (according to the indications present on the products):
- ƙafafun carbon (ƙirar CO2) da marufi (tare da umarnin sake yin amfani da su),
- Nutriscore (ci abinci mai gina jiki), abinci mai gina jiki,/ mai sa jiki, carbohydrates, sugars, fiber, furotin da gishiri da kuma sodium.
- brands, allergens, labels (organic, vegan...)
- A kan ruwan inabi da giya, zaku sami abun ciki na barasa.
