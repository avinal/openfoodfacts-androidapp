Scan pet food products or their barcode to obtain data on ingredients, additives and nutrition facts.

▶ Poiščite in primerjajte več kot 800.000 izdelkov

The app allows to view the over 600 pet food products already contained in the free and open database Open Pet Food Facts, and also to add pictures and data for missing products.


To discover the Open Pet Food Facts project, please visit https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org

*** Prispevajte k preglednosti izdelkov ***
- Open Products Facts is a products database made by everyone, for everyone.
- You can use it to make better choices, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.
- Open Products Facts is a non-profit project developed by thousands of volunteers from around the world. Imamo veliko zanimivih projektov, pri katerih lahko sodelujete na več različnih načinov.
- You can start contributing today by adding your first product.

*** Pridobite dejstva ***
- Open Products Facts helps you to make sense of the fine print on products labels. You will also be able to find (according to the indications present on the products):
- ogljični odtis (emisije CO2) in embalažo (kot tudi navodila za recikliranje),
- Nutriscore (hranilna vrednost), hranila, vsebnost maščobe, nasičene maščobne kisline, ogljikovi hidrati, sladkorji, vlaknine, beljakovine in sol in natrij.
- brands, allergens, labels (organic, vegan...)
- na vinih in pivih boste našli vsebnost alkohola.
